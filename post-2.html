Home Gym on a Budget: What's Actually Worth Buying

Fitness · June 15, 2026

You don't need a rack full of chrome-plated equipment to get fit at home. We tested the cheap stuff so you don't have to guess which gadgets are worth the shelf space.

Adjustable dumbbells beat a full fixed-weight set for almost everyone with limited space. You lose a bit of speed switching weights, but you gain back an entire corner of the room.

Advertisement

Resistance bands are the most underrated piece of equipment in this list. They're cheap, they pack flat, and they cover a surprising range of exercises that would otherwise need a cable machine.

Skip the fancy smart mirror unless you genuinely need the class library. A $20 yoga mat and a phone propped against the wall gets you 90% of the value.
