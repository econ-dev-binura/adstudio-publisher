Five Underrated Towns Worth a Weekend Trip

Travel · June 10, 2026

Skip the crowds. These small towns pack in just as much character as the big-name destinations, minus the lines, the markup, and the wait for a table.

The trick to finding these places is looking one stop past the obvious tourist hub. Towns that feed off a bigger city's tourism, without being the main event themselves, tend to keep prices honest and the pace slow.

Advertisement

Booking midweek instead of the weekend gets you better rates and first pick of restaurant tables, and most of these towns are small enough that a two-day trip covers everything worth seeing.

Bring cash. Smaller towns lean on local card readers that aren't always reliable, and the best food is usually at the stall that doesn't take cards at all.
